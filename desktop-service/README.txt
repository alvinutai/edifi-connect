EDiFi Connect — Desktop Service
================================
Elite Dental Force Inc.

INSTALLATION
------------
1. Extract this entire folder to your Desktop or Documents
2. Double-click install.bat
3. When your browser opens, paste your EDiFi Office Code and click Connect
4. Add the Chrome extension (see CHROME EXTENSION below)

That's it! The service starts automatically when you log into Windows.

GETTING YOUR OFFICE CODE
------------------------
1. Log in to EDiFi (edifi-eligibility-platform.netlify.app)
2. Go to Settings → Office tab
3. Copy the Office ID (looks like: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx)

CHROME EXTENSION
----------------
1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
2. Turn on "Developer mode" (toggle in top-right corner)
3. Click "Load unpacked"
4. Select the "chrome-extension" folder from this package
5. The EDiFi Connect icon will appear in your toolbar

Once installed, log into your dental insurance portals (Delta Dental, MetLife,
etc.) as you normally would. EDiFi Connect captures your session automatically —
no passwords are stored or shared.

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
- Service not running? Double-click install.bat to reinstall
- Not connecting? Check that http://localhost:47821/health shows in your browser
- Need help? Contact support@elitedentalforce.com
